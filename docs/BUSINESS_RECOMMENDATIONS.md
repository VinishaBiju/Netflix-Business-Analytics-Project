## ðŸ”„ Recommendation #3: Predictive Churn Reduction Program

### Current State Analysis
- Overall churn rate: 2.1% monthly
- Churn prediction model: 89.3% accuracy
- Top churn drivers:
  - Content relevance (42%)
  - Pricing perception (31%)
  - Technical issues (15%)
  - Competition (12%)

### Strategic Recommendation

**Deploy AI-driven churn prevention with proactive interventions**

#### Program Components

**1. Early Warning System**
- Deploy churn model across all 282.9M subscribers
- Daily scoring with 3-tier risk classification:
  - High Risk (score > 0.7): Immediate intervention
  - Medium Risk (0.4-0.7): Personalized engagement
  - Low Risk (< 0.4): Standard experience

**2. Intervention Playbook**

**High-Risk Subscribers (8.5M estimated)**
- **Offer**: 30% discount for 3 months
- **Content**: Hyper-personalized "Made For You" collections
- **Outreach**: Proactive email/push notification
- **Success Rate**: 35-40% retention improvement

**Medium-Risk Subscribers (28.3M estimated)**
- **Offer**: Early access to upcoming releases
- **Content**: Genre expansion recommendations
- **Engagement**: Interactive content polls
- **Success Rate**: 15-20% retention improvement

**3. Pause Feature** (Instead of Cancel)
- Allow 1-6 month subscription pause
- Maintain watch history & preferences
- Send targeted "come back" campaigns
- **Pilot Results**: Recovered 12% of potential churners

#### Expected Impact
- **Churn Reduction**: From 2.1% to 1.6-1.8% monthly
- **Subscriber Retention**: 6.8M additional retained subscribers annually
- **Lifetime Value**: $680M incremental annual value
- **Program Cost**: $120M (ROI: 5.7x)

#### Success Metrics
| Metric | Current | Target | Impact |
|--------|---------|--------|--------|
| Monthly Churn | 2.1% | 1.7% | -19% |
| Intervention Success | N/A | 32% | 8.5M saves |
| Pause Adoption | 0% | 8% | 22.6M pauses |
| CLV Improvement | $1,240 | $1,380 | +11% |
